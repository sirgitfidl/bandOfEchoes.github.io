Band of Echoes is an acoustic duo reimagining songs by Tool, Nine Inch Nails, Alice In Chains, Soundgarden, and more. Featuring the intricate interplay of cello and guitar, layered with intertwined male and female vocals, they transform heavy rock into raw, emotional acoustic arrangements. Their stripped-down sound is dark, dynamic, and deeply immersive.
